ABOUT

Hello, my name is Kirsty. I am a web developer who loves to learn new technologies. I am experienced with frontend usage of Javascript, jQuery, React, CSS3, and semantic HTML5. On the backend I'm comfortable with Node, Express and PostgreSQL. Always learning - I'm currently diving deeper into database management and SASS.


I am an avid cyclist and conservationist and enjoy giving back to nature whenever possible. When I'm not enjoying the great outdoors you can find me at my studio in central Massachusetts playing around with Arduinos and Raspberry Pis, 3d printing bits and bobs, or creating surrealist collages.
